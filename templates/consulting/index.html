Consulting service options

  • Private cloud infrastructure
  • App modernization and Kubernetes
  • Data platform and AI/ML
  • Observability
  • Ubuntu for enterprise

Private cloud

We enable open source Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) on your premises, designed according to best practices and optimized for price performance. We build on standards with OpenStack and MicroCloud-based private cloud. We ensure that you get the latest functionality, optimized software-defined Ceph storage, integrations with external storage appliances, state of the art software-defined networking based on OVN. We deliver all of the high availability and disaster recovery functionality you need for the perfect open source private cloud. We also offer OpenStack-based training for both day 1 deployment, and day 2 operations and troubleshooting.

Learn about our OpenStack consulting services ›

MAAS

Automate your bare metal lifecycle management with MAAS. Our team will set up a MAAS cluster to handle the entire process seamlessly. The MAAS cluster will take care of server commissioning, operating system deployment and configuration, networking configuration, and erase and release of any bare metal server.

We offer training on MAAS deployment and operations.

Ceph storage

We design and deploy a multi-tier, software-defined storage cluster based on Ceph that is optimized for price performance. With automated scale out capabilities, it can be the perfect replacement for traditional storage arrays

We also offer full training on Ceph operations and optimization.

Container security

Get long term security patching for your containers and version of choice. We ensure that the latest security patches are always backported to your chosen version, and we provide the ability to secure multiple versions of the same container.

The latest secured image of your chosen container version will always be available to download and use in your production environment.

Kubernetes

We deliver an enterprise-grade Kubernetes deployment based on our 12 year LTS Canonical Kubernetes, with design, deployment, testing, and acceptance on any substrate.

This Kubernetes deployment can run in any environment - from a single board device at the edge, to the biggest scale out cluster in a data center - and it includes the latest integrations with GPU-powered physical and virtual environments.

We provide a full training course on Kubernetes deployment and operation.

Databases

We provide enterprise support and deployment services for the most popular open source databases, such as MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, OpenSearch, and Valkey. We will design and deploy a production-grade open source database on any public, private or hybrid cloud.

We can deliver your database as a standalone deployment, or we can provide you with the functionality to offer self-service deployment to your end users.

We also offer comprehensive training for these database applications.

Kafka

Replace your enterprise edition Kafka with a fully supported open source Kafka. We will design and deploy a production-grade open source Kafka cluster and help you migrate all of your application messaging.

AI/MLOps

Turn your AI use case into reality with the following services:

  • AI workshop – We run a workshop to gather your business and technical requirements and translate them to infrastructure and AI applications, which will lead to a design for your AI training and inference environments.
  • MLOps design – We design your full stack AI environment from the ground up, including platform capacity planning, operating system, container orchestration, Kubeflow, MLflow, KServe, OpenSearch, Kafka, and applications for training and inference.
  • MLOps deployment and operation – We deploy the designed architecture on your choice of substrate, such as private and public clouds, GPU-enabled bare metal clusters, and edge devices.
We also provide classroom training for MLOps operations.

Observability

Replace your expensive observability platform with best-of-breed open source alternatives, such as Grafana, Prometheus, and Loki for metrics visualization, reporting, logging, and alerting. We provide a full design and deployment service for your open source observability stack that you can use to monitor your infrastructure and applications across any environment.

Ubuntu Desktop and Server Enterprise deployments

Are you ready to make Ubuntu a first-class citizen in your organization? We are here to help you make that a reality. From deployment automation, authentication and security hardening and compliance, our services team can help you make your Ubuntu estate enterprise-ready.
