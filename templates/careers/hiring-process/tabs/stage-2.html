Early stage interviews

You will have 3 interviews focused on technical and domain-specific skills. These interviewers are likely to be future colleagues, peers and close stakeholders. The purpose of this stage is to evaluate your technical experience so that we can make a decision on which team and role you best align to.

Talent Interview

You will meet with a member of our Talent Science team so that we can understand your career motivations and journey. To prepare, you will be asked to complete a 10-minute Personality Profile Analysis (PPA) that examines your most typical working style. During this interview we will also start discussions on compensation, helping you understand our philosophy and to align expectations before the final stage.

Late stage interviews

You will meet your potential manager, your Hiring Lead and any other senior stakeholders relevant to the role that you are being considered for. If you are applying for a management or leadership role you will also meet with our CEO.
